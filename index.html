Your Journey, Our Passion

At Coastal Heritage Safaris, we are more than just a team — we’re a vibrant collective of locals and global explorers, bonded by decades of experience across various industries and a shared love for creating unforgettable travel experiences. With deep roots along the Kenyan coast and insider knowledge of its best-kept secrets, we don’t just take you places — we curate immersive journeys that celebrate the culture, beauty, and soul of this stunning region. Today, getting to the coast has never been easier. With direct international flights now arriving from Europe, the Middle East, and Asia — and vastly improved local travel options including the SGR train, modern road networks, and domestic flights — Kenya’s coastal paradise is more accessible than ever. Whether you're planning a family getaway, a romantic escape, a business retreat, or a solo adventure, we’re here to tailor every detail to match your vision. From airport transfers to personalized excursions, we handle everything with passion, precision, and a warm coastal smile..
